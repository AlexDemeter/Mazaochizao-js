Here you can commission an artwork from me.
- first you add your name or whatever nickname you want me to refer to you as.
- next you should add your e-mail address so we can discuss your subject matter further or if you want to reach out to me for anything.
If you sign up for a commission here,
I'll send you an e-mail asking you to reply with your instagram (or facebook) account and references.
Providing me with your instagram makes it so that we have a more flexible way of discussing prices and rates!
-It's also important, because the finished artwork will be sent via the e-mail you provide here for the best quality possible.
- Then for the subject matter, make sure to provide clear information for what you want and the direction you want the piece to go.
-Oh, and one more thing! Transactions through PayPal, I'll provide that for you in the earlier mentioned e-mail stage.
